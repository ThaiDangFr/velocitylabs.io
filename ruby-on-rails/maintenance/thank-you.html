The journey of a thousand miles begins with one step.

Let's get started!

Here are some things you can start doing before we call you for a kickoff meeting.

  • Notify any other project stakeholders that we're planning to have a kickoff meeting.
  • Gather any supporting documentation you may have in regards to your application.
  • Gather your 3rd party credentials (Github/Bitbucket, Heroku/AWS/Rackspace/Hosting provider, etc)
  • Compile a list of your known bugs, we'll import them into a Trello board for your project.
  • Compile a list of small tasks you need help with.
  • Anything else you think will be helpful? Let us know.
  • Pat yourself on the back for getting your project back on track.
